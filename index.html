About Me

My name is Adam Russo, and I've had two passions in life: art and computers. Here, I can merge those passions and show off my creativity with syntax as well as with pigments. This website is made using a combination of HTML, Bootstrap CSS, and JavaScript. As I continue to work on it, I'll add more and more functionality.

Projects

The first project is, of course, this website. I started building it on 2/27/24, and got the navbar, the three sections, and the thumbnail gallery working. The following day, I finished the carousel and added a modal form for email submissions. The hardest part was getting the carousel to open on whichever thumbnail I clicked on, and not just whichever was set to "active." I used ChatGPT, w3schools.com. and stackoverflow.com to help me through the bulk of the hurdles I faced.
